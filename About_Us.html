Our Approach

  • Stage 1: Initiation The project implementation process kicks of with a warm welcome from the SampanIT team. The team will provide an overview of the options, details of technical support, payment procedure and helpful forms. During this stage, the preliminary project requirement will also be discussed.

  • Stage 2: Planning This is where the real works begins! Through a series of meeting and call our team will explore and document your specific needs and requirements. Based on these findings, our team leader will produce a detailed project plan. This project plan is the roadmap to success. Things can also be changed during the execution stage. Our team will always keep in touch with you.

  • Stage 3: Execution and Testing Based on your needs,demand and the project plan our team will go for coding. Through extensive trial and testing we will provide you an bugless software. And this stage does not end until our client is stasified with our product.
